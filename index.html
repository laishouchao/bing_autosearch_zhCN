Need help? (Tutorial and Settings)

"Points need a break:" Under the new Microsoft Rewards rules, for some restricted accounts, points will only be awarded for 3 searches every 15 minutes (If this is your case, use the 5 minute interval.).

Using automation tools is against the Microsoft Rewards terms of use. Your account may be restricted, suspended and/or banned, use this tool at your own risk.

Tutorial
  1. Sign in to Bing with your Microsoft Rewards account and return to this page.
  2. Start a new auto search by clicking the "Start Auto Search" button, the progress counter will be showned.
  3. To perform mobile searches, use your smartphone or emulate a mobile browser through the developer tools (F12) on the PC.
